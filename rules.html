Rules

Classic Rock Paper Scissors game re-imaged with pokemon starters utilizing the poke-api.

Grass defeats Water

Water defeats Fire

Fire defeats Grass

Win a battle to evolve that pokemon.

Evolve all your pokemon to their final (3rd) evolutions to win the game.

If your opponent evolves all their pokemon first, they will win, so be careful!
